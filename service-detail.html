Design & Build

At Above & Beyond Solutions, we excel at making exhibition visions a reality by showcasing our clients' business goals in engaging, impactful ways. Our standout modular builds combine stunning style with smart functionality for maximum brand impact.

SHELL SCHEME

Scoring a shell scheme space at an upcoming show? Let us transform it into something truly eye-catching! Our creative exhibition squad has the skills to elevate your basic shell into an immersive, unforgettable experience.

Our artistic team plans the optimal layout and flow to engage attendees and ensure maximum brand impact. Then we deck out your space with vibrant graphics, signage, displays and more to make your message pop.

When we're done pimping out your ride, your brand will shine loud and clear at the show!

Sustainability

Want an exhibition that's kind to the planet? We're passionate about supporting brands on their journey to achieve their sustainability goals through smart exhibition design.

Our eco-conscious exhibition masters craft stands focused on longevity, reuse, and incorporating green elements. By building exhibitions to a high standard and designing them for easy refurbishing, we create sustainable solutions that reduce waste.

We can't wait to brainstorm ways to showcase your message in an eco-innovative way!

Graphic Design & Print

Want to give your exhibition some extra visual pizzazz? Our graphic design wizards have got you covered!

We help create a lasting brand impression through stylish graphics and signage. From banners and displays to foamex panels and printed props, we design eye-catching elements that make your exhibition pop.

Our top-notch printing capabilities bring designs to life with vibrant colors and quality that lasts. We offer everything from gel edge banners to colored display cases and good old pop-up banners too.

Ticketing

Skip the line and enjoy a hassle-free exhibition experience! Our user-friendly digital ticketing platform lets you conveniently purchase advanced exhibition hall passes online.

We utilize innovative solutions that make ticketing a breeze while preventing fraud. Attendees can simply show their mobile pass, and exhibitors can easily scan tickets at their booths.

By offering advance ticketing, we help drive booth traffic and make it simple for audiences to access the show. Let's chat about how our ticketing tools can create seamless exhibition hall entry and engagement!

Photography & Videography

Take your exhibition visuals to the next level with our rockstar photographers and videographers! We help brands create bold, cinematic content that engages audiences.

From sleek product photos to 360° exhibition footage, we use cutting-edge techniques to fully showcase your brand experience. Our visual storytelling sizzles online and on social media, drawing eyes to your exhibition in innovative ways.

Let's showcase your killer exhibition through stunning photos, videos, and more. Ready for your closeup? Our talent and tech will make you shine!

FURNITURE
